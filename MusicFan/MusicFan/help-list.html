Song Lists

After selecting your sources, you can further refine the contents of a channel within the Song List screen. You can:

  • Reorder
    • You can reorder songs by dragging the drag icon on the right.
  • Delete
    • Delete an item by swiping left and pressing Delete.
  • See Details
    • See the details on an item by swiping left and pressing Details.
  • Load Podcast
    • Load a podcast episode by swiping left and pressing Load. Only loaded podcasts will play.
  • Unload Podcast
    • Unload a podcast episode by swiping left and pressing Unload.
  • Load/Unload UPNP Media
    • You can download a song from a UPNP server by swiping left and pressing Load. If downloaded, the song will play even when the UPNP server is unavailable. You can unload it by swiping left and pressing Unload. UPNP songs will stream even if unloaded, if the UPNP server is available on your network.

    At the bottom are the following control buttons:

    • Sources
      • Add or remove media sources, such as playlists, albums, or radio stations, to the channel. Switches to Sources screen.
    • Refresh
      • For RSS feed channels, contact the RSS feed, and add entries for any newly available podcasts. For regular channels, reevaluate the items in the channel source list , and regenerate the media item list; this will re-randomize the songs as well.
    • DelPrev
      • For RSS feed channels, delete all unloaded podcast entries older than the selected one.
    • Load All
      • For RSS feeds or UPNP server-resident items, download all the items in this channel onto the phone.
    • Done
      • Save changes and return to main screen.
    • Cancel
      • Discard changes and return to main screen. Some changes, such as loading/unloading an RSS item, can't be reverted.
    • Help
      • Display this screen.
