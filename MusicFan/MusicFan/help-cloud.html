Cloud Warning for iOS 8.4

If you stream music from your iTunes library, you must enable the use of the cloud for playing music, as described below. If you don't, AudioGalaxy will get stuck on those items.

On iOS 8.4, first, you must use the latest iTunes (12.2 or later) and enable "Show Apple Music" and "iCloud Music Library" (from iTunes Preferences). Then, sync your iPhone. Then on your iPhone, go to Settings / Music and enable "iCloud Music Library."

On iOS 8.3 and before, go to Settings / Music and enable "Show All Music".
