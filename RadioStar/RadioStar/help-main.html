The RadioStar player lets you search for and play Internet Radio stations. When playing a station, RadioStar remembers the last 30 minutes (by default) played on that station, and you can use the back ("-20") and forward ("+20") buttons to navigate within the already streamed music.

By default, the downloaded cotents of a station are discarded when you select a different station to play. However, if you select "Stream in background" option from the player options while playing a station, RadioStar will continue to stream music while you are playing other stations.
